  • • One place for fun, plans, and growth

We wanted a single, cute place where we can spin wheels to pick a movie or game, track our watch list, keep a shared to-do, save our favorite memories, and study together. It’s simple, modern, and made just for us.

Features

Spin Wheels

Add entries (e.g., Games, Movies) and spin to decide—no more indecision.

Watch List

Track movies/shows to watch next, mark finished, and leave tiny notes.

To-Do List

Quick shared tasks with priority and due dates.

Memories

Drop in clips and screenshots; browse by tags and dates.

Study — Practice

Paste notes → auto-generate flashcards, multiple choice, or written responses. Sequential Q&A (no long scroll), instant feedback with explanations.

How it works

  1. Step 1: Pick a section—Spin, Watch List, To-Do, Memories, or Study.
  2. Step 2: Add your stuff (entries, items, clips, or notes).
  3. Step 3: Use Practice to study your notes with flashcards, MCQs, or written answers—one question at a time with helpful explanations.
